Digital Fog.

An ongoing project and driving force in my photographic journey. Being a collection of images that portray the closest representation of my ideas and dreams. Pictures taken of the rollecoaster that is life, trough happiness and trough sadness, trough laughter and trough out tears. Besides the emotional inspiration, in the past year and counting i have gathered a serious throwback to the past utilising mostly old digital cameras.

The Process.

Make the most out of old cameras. Embrace every imperfection. Corrupt image while still recognizable.
